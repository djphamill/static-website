Distplacement-Time and Velocity-Time Graphs

A particle moves for a minute, starting and finishing at the same point:
  • The first twentry seconds the particle moves for \(2 ms^{-1}\)
  • It remains stationary for the next fifteen seconds.
  • Then for the following fifteen seconds, the particle travels in the opposite direction at \(3 ms^{-1}\)
  • For the remaining time, the particle moves at a constant rate back to its starting position.
  1. Draw a displacement-time graph
  2. What is the particle's velocity in its final stage?
